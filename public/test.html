Discover the exotic beauty of Orange Bay Island on a comfortable boat trip and immerse yourself in the fascinating underwater world of the Red Sea.

Your day begins with pickup from your hotel in Makadi Bay and a relaxing ride in an air-conditioned vehicle to the port. There, a modernly equipped boat awaits, taking you out onto the crystal-clear, azure waters of the Red Sea. Sit back and enjoy the impressive views of the surrounding landscape.

After a short drive, we'll reach a spectacular snorkeling area. Here, you'll have the opportunity to experience the colorful underwater world up close. Put on your snorkel mask and immerse yourself in the fascinating world of coral reefs and exotic marine life that call these clear, blue waters home.

Feel the gentle lapping of the waves as you swim in the refreshing waters of the Red Sea. Enjoy a delicious lunch on board before continuing on to the island.

Once you arrive on Orange Bay Island, a majestic island landscape awaits you. Unwind and enjoy the breathtaking views from the quiet sandy beach, while the gentle sounds of the surf provide pure relaxation.

In the late afternoon, after this unforgettable boat tour, you will return to the harbor, where you will finally be taken to your hotel in Makadi Bay.
